HDD Specialists

Flatirons Drilling Inc was incorporated in February of 2005. Flatirons has worked for numerous contractors and clients such as MasTec, Kahuna Ventures, USI, Flint Energy, Qefs, Antero Resources, Exxon Mobil, Enterprise Production, Kinder Morgan, Level 3, At&t, Xcel, HP and many more.

We have also served a wide variety of industries such as oil, gas, telecommunications, electric, water and sewer. Flatirons takes great pride in all of our projects no matter the scope. We have an impeccable safety record and take the safety of our people very seriously. We have a loyal, tight knit team that has worked for us for a long time, and has a vast amount of experience in what they do. We also have a wide variety of equipment to complete jobs in different areas and terrains, such as crossing rivers, wetlands, mountain sides, downtown streets and narrow back yards.
